⚖️ Key Supreme Court Judgments on RERA

Landmark rulings that define buyer rights in India. Free & open to all visitors.

Supreme Court2019

Pioneer Urban Land & Infrastructure Ltd. v. Union of India

National · IBC & RERA Rights

The Court held that homebuyers qualify as financial creditors under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC), granting them rights under IBC in addition to existing RERA protections.

Key Impact Strengthened buyer remedies against defaulting developers by opening IBC as an additional legal avenue.
View on eSCR Portal ↗
Supreme Court2021

Newtech Promoters and Developers Pvt. Ltd. v. State of UP

National · RERA Jurisdiction

The Supreme Court clarified that RERA authorities have exclusive jurisdiction over disputes relating to delay in possession, refund, and compensation — reducing forum shopping.

Key Impact Ensured consistency in dispute resolution and directed buyers to the dedicated RERA forum.
View on eSCR Portal ↗
Supreme Court2020

M/s Imperia Structures Ltd. v. Anil Patni

National · Consumer Forum Rights

The Court held that RERA remedies are not exclusive — buyers retain the right to approach consumer forums even when RERA remedies are available, expanding redressal options.

Key Impact Expanded buyer options for grievance redressal by preserving consumer forum access alongside RERA.
View on eSCR Portal ↗
Supreme Court2019

Bikram Chatterji v. Union of India (Amrapali Case)

National · Developer Accountability

The Court cancelled the RERA registration of Amrapali Group projects due to mismanagement and diversion of funds, directed NBCC to complete construction, and ordered forensic audits of accounts.

Key Impact Landmark ruling ensuring developer accountability and protecting thousands of stranded homebuyers in NCR.
View on eSCR Portal ↗
Supreme Court2021

Supertech Ltd. v. Emerald Court Owner Resident Welfare Association

National · Building Norms Compliance

The Supreme Court ordered the demolition of twin towers in Noida, citing violations of building norms and RERA provisions. Authorities who granted approvals were also held accountable.

Key Impact Reinforced strict compliance with approvals and set a strong precedent for transparency under RERA.
View on eSCR Portal ↗
