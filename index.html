Our Mission

The mission of PBFI Welfare Association is to protect and promote the rights and interests of property buyers. We aim to create awareness about legal rights, ensure transparency in real estate transactions, and support buyers in resolving issues related to property purchases. Through collective representation, guidance, and advocacy, PBFI Welfare Association works to build a fair, trustworthy, and responsible real estate environment for all property buyers.

Our Vision

With the vision to protect the interests of property buyers, PBFI Welfare Association strives to create a transparent, fair, and accountable real estate environment. We aim to empower property buyers with knowledge, support, and collective representation, ensuring their rights are respected and safeguarded while promoting ethical practices within the real estate sector.
